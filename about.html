About KCEA

We build joyful, lasting experiences — a continuation of the 16‑bit spirit for a new generation — centered on physical play, true ownership, and creators first.

Mission: A Million Smiles

More valuable than a million dollars is a million smiles.

We measure our success by delight — the couch‑co‑op laughter, the late‑night perseverance, the moments you’ll remember years from now. Profit keeps the lights on; smiles light the way. Everything we make strives to be fun to play, beautiful to hold, and worth sharing.

Preserve Physical Games

Games are culture worth preserving. That’s why the KGx Numen platform embraces physical cartridges and tangible packaging. When you own a game, you should own the whole game — playable tomorrow, displayable forever.

We champion permanence in an age of disappearing downloads.

User Ownership

Discs and cartridges live on your shelf, not behind a password. We prioritize designs that respect player ownership: no endless grinds to unlock what should be there, no pay‑to‑win schemes — just complete experiences from the moment you plug in.

Simple, Affordable Pathways

Great ideas shouldn’t be locked behind closed doors. Our developer pathways lower the barrier to entry with practical tooling, documentation, and approachable licensing. Whether you’re a hobbyist or a studio, you’ll find a way to build for Numen without guesswork.

Create. Ship. Hold it in your hands.

Encouraging Creation

We believe players can be makers. From sample projects to dev kits and cartridge‑first distribution, we encourage experimentation and shareable knowledge. The more creators there are, the more worlds there are to explore.
