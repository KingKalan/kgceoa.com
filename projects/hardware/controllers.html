Input Devices

Controllers

Classic style with modern nostalgia — the input devices that make the KGx Numen feel like it came from another era.

While wireless controllers add convenience to modern systems, the Numen embraces wired connections to preserve something deeper. The tug of a cord across the carpet, the scramble when a friend trips the line, the crowded couch filled with laughter and frustration — these shared experiences defined an era of gaming. Both the good and the bad of physically connected controllers shaped how we played together, and that legacy is why the following accessories exist.

Classic Controller

Styled for modern nostalgia, the Numen’s classic controller delivers the feel of a bygone age while offering the precision expected today. It uses traditional communication through proprietary connectors, reinforcing the sensation that this console truly belongs to the 16‑bit era reborn.

Lightgun

The return of the lightgun accessory — built in era‑accurate form, traditionally operated, and designed to bring back that unmistakable style of play. Disclaimer: As with original lightguns, this accessory does not function as intended on most modern televisions. It is meant for use with CRT displays to preserve authentic gameplay.

Multitap

Expanding local multiplayer, the so‑called “multitap” (term subject to legal review) allows more players to connect to a single system. This accessory opens the door to the couch‑sharing, competitive, and cooperative experiences that defined an era of gaming together.
