KGx Numen — Dev Kit

The mothership of the Numen system — built to speed up development. Accessible through our developer pathway.

Licensing remains focused on originality, completeness, and quality — with financially accessible routes for approved teams and creators.

What the Dev Kit Enables

  • Hot‑reload of code/assets to iterate without full rebuilds.
  • On‑target debugging with breakpoints, memory/VRAM inspectors.
  • Real‑time serial logging and diagnostics overlays.
  • Performance captures and frame‑time profiling.
  • Asset pipeline tools for sprites, tiles, maps, and audio.
  • Scriptable build system for reproducible releases.
  • Cycle‑accurate emulator to complement on‑hardware testing.
  • Network deploy to console over USB/Ethernet.
  • Save‑state, rewind, and deterministic replays for QA.
  • Crash‑dump capture with symbolized stack traces.
  • Automated test runner for gameplay/regression suites.
  • Dev menu on hardware for toggles, cheats, and sandboxing.

Includes SDK docs, sample projects, and code templates to get teams productive quickly — without disclosing proprietary internals.
