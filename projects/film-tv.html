In the Hours Between — Screenplay in Development

The story that begins as a 16‑bit adventure is being reimagined for film. In the Hours Between follows a young hero rising in a world caught between twilight and dawn, where shadows spread and hope flickers faintly. As allies gather and secrets are uncovered, love and courage become the only forces strong enough to push back the darkness.

The screenplay aims to capture the spirit of the game: heartfelt storytelling, mystery‑rich exploration, and the timeless struggle between light and dark. With a focus on character‑driven drama and epic cinematic moments, the adaptation will serve as both a faithful expansion of the source material and a standalone narrative for new audiences.

Just as the game preserves the legacy of the 16‑bit era, the screenplay preserves the heart of adventure storytelling, bridging retro inspiration with modern cinematic vision.
