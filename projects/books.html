The definitive telling of the story of Cynoria and Serophis, and their struggle against the Dawnless King and his minions for the fate of Farithos.

In the Hours Between — Complete Prologue

This book represents the full narrative form of the world first glimpsed in In the Hours Between. Where the game hints and the screenplay expands, the novel offers the definitive version of events — tracing the rise of Cynoria and Serophis as they confront the encroaching darkness threatening to erase the dawn.

The pages chronicle the perilous journey across Farithos, the alliances formed, and the sacrifices demanded as the Dawnless King unleashes his armies. It is here that the mythos finds its most complete telling, capturing both the grandeur of epic battles and the intimate moments of love and resolve that define the heroes.

For readers and players alike, this novel stands as the cornerstone of the saga — a preservation of lore, character, and story in its most enduring form.
