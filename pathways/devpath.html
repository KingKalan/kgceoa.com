Developer Pathways

Two distinct routes to create on the KGx Numen platform — from indie homebrew to officially licensed titles.

Both pathways honor openness and creativity while safeguarding quality standards, ensuring every release on the Numen is complete, original, and true to the legacy of the platform.

Homebrew Pathway

Elevate privileges on your system to flash deployable ROMs directly to cartridges, enabling development and testing on real Numen hardware. Intended as a lower‑tier developer pathway for hobbyists and indie creators.

Licensed Developer

The official pathway for studios seeking to develop and deploy licensed titles at scale. Supports the full pipeline for bringing complete, polished games to market on the Numen system.
